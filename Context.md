# QCXIS Sdn Bhd — Context

---

## Strategic Rationale

QC Group Sdn Bhd is in the process of formalising **QCXIS Sdn Bhd** as its dedicated IT subsidiary, responsible for delivering technology infrastructure, platform development, and IT operations across the franchise network. This restructuring is a deliberate step toward separating IT as a distinct, self-sustaining business unit with its own operational accountability.

### Employment Restructuring

A core component of this initiative is the **transfer of employment** for selected IT staff from QC Group Sdn Bhd to QCXIS Sdn Bhd. Rather than a one-time transfer, this will be executed in phases — beginning with two staff members — and scaled progressively as QCXIS demonstrates the financial capacity to support its own payroll independently.

### Why the Forecast & Roadmap Exist

To determine the feasibility and timing of this, a **Business Forecast and Infrastructure Roadmap** covering 2026 to 2031 was developed. The forecast models revenue projections across three scenarios — Conservative, Optimum, and Aggressive — based on the RM1 per active client per month fee structure and a targeted outlet growth from 226 to 500 locations. The roadmap also incorporates staffing cost projections, allowing the board to identify clear milestones at which QCXIS can absorb additional headcount without financial risk to the group.

### Unified Purpose

All preceding work — the forecast, the roadmap, the SLA documentation, and the governance frameworks — serves a unified purpose: to build QCXIS Sdn Bhd into a **credible, structured, and financially viable entity** capable of operating as a proper subsidiary, with its own staff, its own service agreements, and its own accountability to QC Group.
