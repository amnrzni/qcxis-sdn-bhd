# QCXIS Sdn Bhd — Company Profile

---

## Overview

QCXIS Sdn Bhd is a technology company specializing in SaaS solutions for individuals, startups, and established organizations. Originally formed as the IT subsidiary of QC Group Sdn Bhd — a private education franchise network in Malaysia — QCXIS develops and operates platforms built with multi-tenancy and distributed operations at their core, serving organizations that manage people and processes across multiple locations, teams, or entities.

---

## Vision

To be the ecosystem where organizations — from single teams to multi-location networks — manage their workforce, operations, and everyday work life in one connected space.

## Mission

To build a secure, modular SaaS ecosystem designed for organizations that operate across multiple locations, teams, or entities — centralizing workforce management, operations, and collaboration to drive efficiency and sustainable growth.
