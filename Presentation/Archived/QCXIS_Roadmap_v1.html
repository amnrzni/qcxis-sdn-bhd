Infrastructure (QC Group-fronted)
QC Group purchases and owns all major infrastructure (servers, networking, server room, DC hosting). QCXIS pays a fixed monthly management fee (TBD). Starting 2028, QCXIS repurchases assets — transferring ownership to its own balance sheet.
Management (QCXIS-owned)
All people costs — payroll, statutory contributions, insurance, benefits — are QCXIS' direct responsibility. Staff are employed by QCXIS, transferred from QC Group in phases.
