Note for Finance: CTC at each tier follows a single formula — base + RM 450 fixed allowance, multiplied by the 14.95% statutory loading (EPF 13% employer + SOCSO 1.75% + EIS 0.2%), plus RM 150 group medical insurance. From Jan-2028 all roles are eligible for a +5% annual uplift, but the uplift is now profit-gated: it fires on 1 January only if the prior calendar year closed with a positive pre-tax Net. A loss year freezes the multiplier; the next profitable year resumes +5% on top of the last applied. Tier-3 base of RM 5,000 specifically meets the MSC Digital MD-Status knowledge-worker monthly salary floor; achievement unlocks Portrait module 14% concessionary tax (vs the 15/17/24% SME graduated bands).
